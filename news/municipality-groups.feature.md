Restrict the SSO-apps user sync to members of organisation-specific municipality groups. The allowed groups are stored as a new `municipality_groups` property on the `oidc_sso_apps` plugin, populated at startup from the `SSO_APPS_MUNICIPALITY_GROUPS` environment variable (e.g. `[pl_belleville_ac]`) and editable from the SSO Apps control panel. When empty, all access-group members are imported as before. [remdub]
